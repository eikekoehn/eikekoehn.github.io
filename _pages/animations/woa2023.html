Climatological distributions of ocean properties (World Ocean Atlas 2023)

These visualizations show the global distribution of temperature, salinity, dissolved oxygen, nitrate, phosphate and N-star from the World Ocean Atlas 2023 (Reagan et al., 2024). Maps show temperature and salinity at the sea surface and oxygen averaged vertically over the upper 1000m. Vertical sections are placed on the Equator. Note the different depth spacing between the upper and lower 500m (1000m) of the water column. Ocean bathymetry data is taken from the Global Ocean Physics Reanalysis (CMEMS, 2025). Land topography is based on ETOPO5 data (National Geophysical Data Center, 1993).
