Hi there, I am a physical oceanographer by training, who has ventured into the world of marine biogeochemistry. I am currently working as a Postdoctoral researcher at LOCEAN, where I investigate tipping points in Earth System Models. Before coming to Paris, I did my PhD at ETH Zurich (in the group of Prof. Nicolas Gruber) on marine extreme events, such as marine heatwaves and extreme oxygen minimum zone variability.

  • my research interests
