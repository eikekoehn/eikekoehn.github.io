My name is Eike E. Köhn and I am a physical oceanographer by training, who has ventured into the world of marine biogeochemistry. I am currently working as a PostDoc in the Environmental Physics lab of Prof. Nicolas Gruber at ETH Zürich, where I continue to investigate marine extreme events using a regional ocean model, which was also the topic of my recently finished PhD thesis.

On this website you can find information about my scientific path, my research interests, and my publications.
